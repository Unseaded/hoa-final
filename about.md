The Meadows of Wright Farm is a picturesque residential community located in Springfield Township, Ohio. Developed on the historic grounds of the former Wright family horse farm, our neighborhood embraces its equestrian heritage through its architecture, landscaping, and the names of our three distinct streets: **Appaloosa Court**, **Thoroughbred Lane**, and **Clydesdale Way**.

### Our History

For over fifty years, the Wright Farm was a premier breeding ground for fine horses. When the area was developed into a residential community, care was taken to preserve the rolling hills, mature trees, and the warm, welcoming spirit of the original estate. Subtle architectural details throughout the neighborhood serve as a gentle nod to this rich past.

### Our Mission

The Meadows of Wright Farm Homeowners Association is dedicated to preserving the beauty, safety, and property values of our community. We strive to foster a friendly neighborhood atmosphere, maintain our shared amenities, and uphold the covenants that make our community a wonderful place to call home.
