### Frequently Asked Questions

**When is trash and recycling pickup?**
Trash is collected every Tuesday morning. Recycling is collected every other Tuesday. Please have your bins at the curb by 7:00 AM.

**How do I pay my annual HOA dues?**
Dues are collected annually in January. You can mail a check to the Management Company address on the Contact page, or pay online via the Apex Management Portal.

**Do I need permission to paint my house or build a fence?**
Yes. Any exterior modifications require approval from the Architectural Review Committee (ARC). Please download the ARC Request Form from the [Documents page](/documents/) and submit it prior to beginning work.
