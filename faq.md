### When is trash and recycling collected?

Trash and recycling are collected every **Tuesday morning**. Please ensure your bins are placed at the curb by 6:00 AM on collection day, and returned to your garage or behind your house by Tuesday evening.

### Do I need approval for exterior changes to my home?

Yes. Any structural changes, paint color modifications, fence installations, or significant landscaping updates must be approved by the Architectural Review Committee (ARC). Please submit an ARC Request Form (available on the [Documents](/documents/) page) before beginning any work.

### How do I pay my annual HOA dues?

Annual dues are collected by our management company. You can mail a check to the address listed on our [Contact](/contact/) page, or pay online through the management portal. Dues are payable by January 31st each year.

### Who is responsible for maintaining the split-rail fences?

The HOA maintains the split-rail fencing along the main entrances. Homeowners are responsible for maintaining any fencing on their individual properties.
